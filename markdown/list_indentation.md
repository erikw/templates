# Markdown lists indentation

Reference: https://stackoverflow.com/a/53867805/265508

## Unordered
* 1 space doesn't works:
 * **told'ya!**

* 2 spaces works:
  * **see!**

* 3 spaces works:
   * **see!**

* 4 spaces works:
    * **see!**


## Unordered
1. 1 space doesn't works:
 1. **told'ya!**

1. 2 spaces doesn't works:
  1. **told'ya!**

1. 3 spaces works:
   1. **see!**

1. 4 spaces works:
    1. **see!**

## Conclusion
Options are:
* Mimimum space for each
  * Unordered: 2 spaces
  * Ordered: 3 spaces
* Consistency; 3 spaces for either
